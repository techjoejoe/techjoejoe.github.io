Escape Room: Hacker Takeover

An innovative surprise gamified training experience that transforms presentation skills development into an exciting digital/physical escape room adventure on iPads. Learners must gather knowledge, crack codes, and complete challenges to demonstrate presentation skills and squash a hacker's bugs while mastering essential communication techniques.

🎮 Gamification 🎭 Immersive Learning 🎤 Presentation Skills 🧩 Problem-Solving

Project Overview

Revolutionizing corporate training through immersive gameplay and narrative-driven learning

The Challenge: Traditional presentation skills training often fails to engage learners, resulting in poor retention and limited skill transfer to real-world scenarios. Organizations needed a more engaging approach that would capture attention and drive lasting behavioral change.

The Solution: I designed and developed a digital physical hybrid escape room experience that seamlessly integrates presentation skills training into an engaging narrative. Learners become agents trying to stop a hacker company takeover, gathering knowledge and practicing application of their communication skills as tools to solve puzzles and save the day.

The Innovation: By combining iPads and physical elements of game mechanics with learning objectives, this solution transforms training into an exciting, competitive experience. The high-stakes scenario creates authentic pressure situations where learners practice skills in context, while competing against their peers.
